Mince Recipe

Description

Yesterday I didn't what to have for supper but I had beef mince meat and leftover potatoes then I check the app and saw simple recipe we can enjoy me and my mom for dinner.

Ingredients

  • 500g beef mince meat
  • 2 potatoes
  • 1/2 onion
  • 1/2 beef stock
  • 1 TSP paprika
  • 1 chicken cube stock
  • 1 tsp rajah
  • 1 tsp BBQ
  • 1 tsp paprika
  • 15 ml cooking oil
  • 1 tsp steak and chop spice
  • 3 tbs brown onion

Steps

  1. Chop onion and assure it on canola oil, then followed by beef stock and mince meat. Let it brown a little then add the sauces, tomato, sweet chilli and the marinate then stir and cook.
  2. Add your mixed vegetables and stir let it cook
  3. Fetch those boiled leftovers potatoes and peel and diced them and add it to the mince
  4. Take a cup and mix the soups the vegetable tomato and brown onion in water then pour it over the mince meat
  5. Your mince stew is cooked ready to be served. Mine is served with rice enjoy
